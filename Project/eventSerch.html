The Bowl of Embers

Host Name

The Bowl of Embers is a level 20 trial. It requires a light party of 4 players. In The Bowl of Embers, players will face the fiery primal Ifrit. The trial is unlocked through main story quests.

The Howling Eye

Host Name

The Howling Eye is a level 44 - 46 trial that requires the player to defeat the primal Garuda. It requires a light party of 4 players. The Howling Eye is unlocked through main story quests
